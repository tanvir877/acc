Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about contacting and visiting Adamjee Cantonment College.

What are the office hours for administrative departments?

Most administrative offices at Adamjee Cantonment College are open from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Sunday through Thursday. Offices are closed on Fridays, Saturdays, and public holidays. Some departments may have different hours, so it's always a good idea to check with the specific department before visiting.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

Campus tours are available Monday through Thursday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. To schedule a tour, please contact the Admissions Office at (123) 456-7891 or email admissions@adamjee.edu at least 48 hours in advance. Group tours for schools or organizations should be scheduled at least two weeks in advance.

Is parking available for visitors?

Yes, visitor parking is available on campus. Visitors can park in designated visitor parking areas located near the main entrance. A visitor parking pass can be obtained from the security office at the main gate. Please note that parking is limited, especially during peak hours, so it's advisable to arrive early.

How long does it typically take to receive a response to an inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 48 business hours. However, during peak periods such as admission season or the beginning of a new semester, response times may be slightly longer. For urgent matters, we recommend calling the relevant department directly.

Can I meet with a faculty member or department head?

Yes, meetings with faculty members or department heads can be arranged by contacting the respective department directly. It's recommended to email or call in advance to schedule an appointment, as faculty members have teaching and research commitments. The Academic Affairs office can also assist in connecting you with the appropriate faculty member.
