Department of Physics

Exploring the fundamental laws of the universe

The Department of Physics offers comprehensive programs that explore the fundamental principles governing the universe, from subatomic particles to cosmic structures.

Our faculty members are engaged in cutting-edge research across various fields of physics, providing students with opportunities to participate in groundbreaking discoveries.

  • Quantum Physics and Field Theory
  • Condensed Matter Physics
  • Astrophysics and Cosmology
  • Nuclear and Particle Physics
  • Applied Physics and Instrumentation
Learn More

Department of Chemistry

Understanding matter and its transformations

The Department of Chemistry is dedicated to advancing knowledge in the molecular sciences and training the next generation of chemists through rigorous academic programs and research opportunities.

Our state-of-the-art laboratories and experienced faculty provide an ideal environment for exploring the fascinating world of chemical reactions and material properties.

  • Organic and Inorganic Chemistry
  • Physical Chemistry and Spectroscopy
  • Analytical Chemistry
  • Biochemistry and Molecular Biology
  • Materials Science and Nanotechnology
Learn More

Department of Biology

Studying life and living organisms

The Department of Biology offers comprehensive programs that explore the diversity, structure, function, and evolution of living organisms from the molecular to the ecosystem level.

Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical laboratory experience, field studies, and research opportunities to prepare students for careers in biological sciences.

  • Molecular and Cell Biology
  • Genetics and Genomics
  • Ecology and Environmental Biology
  • Microbiology and Immunology
  • Plant and Animal Sciences
Learn More

Department of Mathematics

The language of science and logic

The Department of Mathematics provides a strong foundation in mathematical theory, computational techniques, and applications across various scientific disciplines.

Our programs emphasize analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, and the ability to formulate and solve complex mathematical models for real-world problems.

  • Pure Mathematics
  • Applied Mathematics
  • Statistics and Probability
  • Mathematical Modeling
  • Computational Mathematics
Learn More

Department of Computer Science

Advancing technology through computation

The Department of Computer Science offers cutting-edge programs that prepare students for careers in the rapidly evolving field of computing and information technology.

Our curriculum covers theoretical foundations, practical programming skills, and specialized areas such as artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity.

  • Algorithms and Data Structures
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Database Systems and Big Data
  • Computer Networks and Security
  • Software Engineering and Development
Learn More

Department of Engineering and Drawing

Visualizing and designing the future

The Department of Engineering and Drawing focuses on developing technical drawing skills, spatial visualization abilities, and engineering design principles essential for various scientific and technical fields.

Our programs combine traditional drafting techniques with modern computer-aided design tools to prepare students for careers in engineering, architecture, and technical illustration.

  • Technical Drawing and Drafting
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD)
  • Engineering Graphics
  • 3D Modeling and Visualization
  • Design Principles and Applications
Learn More
