Dr. Robert Chen

Professor and Head of Department

Dr. Robert Chen has been leading the Department of Physics since 2018. He received his Ph.D. in Theoretical Physics from MIT and has over 20 years of experience in research and academia.

His research focuses on quantum field theory and its applications to particle physics.
