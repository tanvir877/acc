Join Our Community

Welcome to the Adamjee Cantonment College admissions page. We are delighted that you are considering joining our vibrant academic community. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who demonstrate academic potential, intellectual curiosity, and a commitment to personal growth.

We believe in providing equal opportunities to all deserving students and strive to make our admissions process transparent and accessible. Our diverse student body enriches the learning experience and prepares students for success in a global society.

We encourage you to explore our programs, visit our campus, and connect with our admissions team to learn more about the opportunities available at Adamjee Cantonment College.

Admission Requirements

To be considered for admission to Adamjee Cantonment College, applicants must meet the following requirements:

Academic Qualifications

For undergraduate programs, applicants must have completed HSC/A-Level or equivalent with a minimum GPA of 3.5. For graduate programs, a bachelor's degree from a recognized institution with a minimum CGPA of 3.0 is required.

Required Documents

Applicants must submit the following documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of national ID card or passport
  • Letter of recommendation (for graduate programs)

Entrance Examination

All applicants are required to take an entrance examination that tests their knowledge in relevant subjects. The examination is designed to assess the applicant's aptitude and readiness for the chosen program.

Interview

Shortlisted applicants will be invited for an interview with the admissions committee. The interview is an opportunity for the committee to assess the applicant's motivation, communication skills, and fit for the program.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Adamjee Cantonment College is committed to making education accessible to all deserving students. We offer a range of scholarships and financial aid options to support students in their academic journey.

Merit Scholarship

Award Amount
Up to 100% tuition waiver
Eligibility
Students with outstanding academic performance (GPA 4.5 or above)
Duration
Renewable each semester based on academic performance

The Merit Scholarship is awarded to students who demonstrate exceptional academic achievement. Recipients are selected based on their academic records, entrance examination scores, and interview performance.

Learn More

Need-Based Financial Aid

Award Amount
Up to 50% tuition waiver
Eligibility
Students with demonstrated financial need
Duration
Renewable each semester based on continued need and academic standing

The Need-Based Financial Aid program is designed to support students who have the academic potential but lack the financial resources to pursue higher education. Applicants must submit financial documentation to demonstrate need.

Learn More

Sports Scholarship

Award Amount
Up to 75% tuition waiver
Eligibility
Students with exceptional sports achievements at national or international level
Duration
Renewable each semester based on sports performance and academic standing

The Sports Scholarship is awarded to students who have demonstrated exceptional talent and achievement in sports. Recipients are expected to represent the college in sports competitions while maintaining satisfactory academic performance.
