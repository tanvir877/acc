About Our Library

The Adamjee Cantonment College Library is a center for academic excellence and intellectual growth. Our library provides a conducive environment for learning, research, and collaboration.

With extensive collections of books, journals, and digital resources, we aim to support the academic needs of our students and faculty.

Library Rules

Silence Must Be Maintained

Students must maintain silence in the library at all times to create a conducive environment for reading and studying.

Library Card is Mandatory

Students must carry their library card at all times and present it when borrowing books or using library facilities.

Book Borrowing Period

Books can be borrowed for a period of 14 days. A maximum of 3 books can be borrowed at a time.

Late Return Penalties

Late returns will incur a fine of 5 Taka per day per book. Continued late returns may result in suspension of borrowing privileges.

Handling of Books

Books must be handled with care. Marking, folding pages, or damaging books in any way is strictly prohibited.

Lost or Damaged Books

Students will be required to replace lost or damaged books or pay the current market price of the book plus a processing fee.

Facilities of ACC Library

Reading Areas

Spacious and well-lit reading areas with comfortable seating for individual study and research.

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with internet access for research, assignments, and accessing digital resources.

Wi-Fi Connectivity

High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the library for students to access online resources.

Printing & Photocopying

Printing and photocopying services available at nominal charges for academic purposes.

Group Study Rooms

Dedicated rooms for group discussions and collaborative learning projects.

Digital Repository

Access to digital journals, e-books, and online databases for comprehensive research.
