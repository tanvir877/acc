Our Story

Adamjee Cantonment College was established in 1960 with a vision to provide quality education to the students of Bangladesh. Over the years, we have grown into one of the premier educational institutions in the country, known for our academic excellence and commitment to holistic development.

Our institution is built on the foundation of strong values, innovative teaching methodologies, and a supportive learning environment. We believe in nurturing not just academic brilliance but also character, creativity, and leadership skills in our students.

Today, Adamjee Cantonment College stands as a symbol of educational excellence, producing graduates who go on to make significant contributions in various fields both nationally and internationally.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to excel in their chosen fields and contribute meaningfully to society. We are committed to creating a learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and ethical leadership.

We strive to make education accessible to all deserving students regardless of their socio-economic background and to promote research and innovation that addresses real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized nationally and internationally for academic excellence, innovative teaching methodologies, and impactful research. We envision creating global citizens who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values to lead and transform society.

We aspire to be at the forefront of educational innovation, setting new standards for teaching and learning while preserving our rich cultural heritage and values.

Our History

1960

Foundation

Adamjee Cantonment College was established with a small campus and limited programs, focusing primarily on providing quality education to the local community.

1975

Expansion

The college expanded its campus and introduced new academic programs to meet the growing demand for quality education in various fields.

1985

Recognition

The college received national recognition for its academic excellence and contribution to the education sector in Bangladesh.

1995

Modernization

The college underwent significant modernization, upgrading its infrastructure, laboratories, and teaching methodologies to keep pace with global standards.

2005

Digital Transformation

The college embraced digital technology, integrating it into its teaching and administrative processes to enhance efficiency and effectiveness.

2015

Golden Jubilee

The college celebrated its 50th anniversary, marking half a century of excellence in education and service to the nation.

2025

Future Vision

The college continues to evolve, with plans for further expansion, international collaborations, and innovative programs to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.
